### üî¨ Key Projects

#### üå≤ Transcriptomics of *Pinus sylvestris* (Scots Pine)
*De novo* transcriptome assembly and functional annotation to study cytogenetic parameters under industrial pollution.
* **Scale:** Processed and assembled **18 samples** *de novo*.
* **Pipeline:** QC $\rightarrow$ Assembly (Trinity) $\rightarrow$ Annotation $\rightarrow$ Analysis.
* [Link to Repository](https://github.com/anapomerash/Pinus_sylvestris_transcriptome)

#### üå≥ Xylogenesis in *Betula pendula* (Karelian Birch)
Comprehensive RNA-Seq analysis to identify molecular mechanisms of patterned wood formation.
* **Scale:** Assembly of **13 transcriptomes** (*B. pendula* & *var. carelica*) and reference-based mapping of **60 samples**.
* **Results:** Identified **1,103 DEGs** associated with programmed cell death and stress response.
* **Data:** Submitted to NCBI BioProject ([PRJNA1173919](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/bioproject/PRJNA1173919), [PRJNA1328362](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/bioproject/PRJNA1328362)).
